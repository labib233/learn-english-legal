Learn English Account Deletion

Last updated: March 30, 2026

In-app deletion

If you are signed in, you can request account deletion from the profile area inside the Learn English app.

Deleting an account is intended to remove the learner account together with linked synced data such as profile details, progress, sessions, subscriptions, and community records.

Public deletion request page

This page is the public deletion resource that can be shared in Google Play.
