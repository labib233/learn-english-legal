# Learn English Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

## What we collect

Learn English may collect learner profile details such as name, email address, English level, goal, and accent preference when you create an account or update your profile.

The app stores learning progress such as completed lessons, exam scores, XP, streaks, review items, and recent activity so learners can continue where they left off.

If speaking practice is used, the app may also process microphone recordings, transcripts, coach feedback, and session history. Optional community features may also store classrooms joined, posts, room messages, and live-room participation.

## How we use data

We use learner data to deliver courses, save progress, sync study history across devices, and support optional account features such as classroom participation and cloud backup.

If a live coach API is connected, voice recordings and transcript data may be sent to trusted processing providers only for speech transcription, coaching, and speaking feedback.

## Storage and sharing

Account and sync data are stored in Supabase when sign-in is enabled. Optional speaking and billing integrations may use additional providers that process only the information required for those features.

Learn English is designed not to sell personal data. Data is shared only with service providers needed to run the app experience, such as authentication, storage, billing, or speech-processing services.

## Retention and deletion

Local study data remains on the device until the app is removed, the app state is reset, or the learner deletes their account.

Cloud data remains available until the learner requests deletion or deletes their account in the app. Account deletion is intended to remove the learner account together with linked profile, progress, session, subscription, and community records.

## Your choices

Creating an account is optional for course study. Learners can use the main course experience without signing in and can sign in later if they want cloud sync.

Learners can request account deletion inside the app when signed in. Public privacy policy and deletion-request URLs should also be published in the relevant app stores.
